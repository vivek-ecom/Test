Coffee Recipe
Mini Mocha Ganache Cakes
These rich, decadent desserts will make your friends and family swoon. Don't be fooled by their diminutive size - these mini cakes pack a chocolaty punch that will knock out even the most demanding chocolate lover. Serve with espresso or cappuccino for an elegant end to your next dinner party.
Ingredients
For the cakes:
  • 1 cup (250 ml) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (250 ml) unsalted butter
  • 3 eggs, plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) vanilla
  • ½ cup (125 ml) cocoa powder
  • 2 cups (500 ml) cake flour
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) baking powder
  • 1 tbsp (15 ml) baking soda
  • ½ tsp (3 ml) salt
  • 1 cups (250 ml) sour cream
  • ½ cup (125 ml) brewed coffee, cooled
For the ganache:
  • 8 oz (1 package) unsweetened chocolate, chopped, melted and cooled slightly
  • 1 cup (250 ml) unsalted butter
  • ½ cup (125 ml) whipping cream
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) vanilla
  • 2 ½ cups (625 ml) icing sugar
Directions

In a medium-sized bowl, beat sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla for two minutes on medium speed. In a large bowl, stir together the flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring with a spoon just until blended. Then beat the mixture for one minute on low speed. Beat in the sour cream and coffee. Spoon into 8 4" ramekins, filling each to about ¾ full.

Bake in centre of 350°F (180°C) oven for 18 to 22 minutes. Cool on racks for 15 minutes. Remove from ramekins; let cool completely on racks.

Ganache: Chop chocolate into small pieces and melt over low heat; cool slightly. In bowl, beat together butter and cream on high speed. Beat in vanilla. Gradually beat in icing sugar.

Place cooled cakes on parchment paper. Ice cakes with warm ganache. Decorate with shortbread or berries. Transfer cakes onto plates to serve.
